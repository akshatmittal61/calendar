chmonth() : function
This function assigns mon the dates of the month from 1-31.
Every value of array Day is emptied first.
Then we check whether year is leap year or not.
After that, extra dates are popped out of array mon.
The head head of the calendar is then colored according to month.

chdate() : function
This function first empties every cell in the body of table.
Then it assigns every date its day accordingly.
After that, prcode assigns a cell to respective dates
for confirmation we print every date with its day in console.
today's date is colored with the theme color in background.
